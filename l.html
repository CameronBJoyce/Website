A Love Letter to Lavender



“I think it pisses God off if you walk by the color purple in a field somewhere and don't notice it. People think pleasing God is all God cares about. But any fool living in the world can see it always trying to please us back.”
- Alice Walker



And still, after all this time, The sun never says to the earth, 'You owe Me.'
Look what happens with A love like that, It lights the Whole Sky.
- Hafez



I really like lavender. The purple in the fields always reminds me that life and little worlds exist outside of my own.



The purple flowers springing by the drainage ditch is a sign of not only life, but of beauty. It can break dreary landscapes, attract bees and butterflies, and repel pests. These are wonderful flowers that have been utilized for thousands of years for differing purposes. Stop and smell some purple for me next time you have the pleasure of seeing some. I know I will.


I will update this page once I finally am able to start growing bushels of my own.
