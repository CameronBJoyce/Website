Sad Trombone Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7th, 2025

1. Introduction

Sad Trombone (“the Extension”) is a Chrome browser extension that plays a “wah-wah” sound effect on user-defined websites. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension collects, uses, and protects your information. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Data Collection and Storage

  • Stored Domains: The Extension stores the domains you choose (e.g., example.com) using Chrome Sync or local storage. This is used solely to determine when to trigger the sound effect.
  • No Personal Information: The Extension does not collect, process, or store any personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email, billing info).
  • No External Transmission: All data remains within your browser. The Extension does not transmit data to any servers or third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Sound Activation: The Extension checks the current hostname against your saved domains. If it matches, a trombone sound plays on user interaction (click or keypress).
  • Options Management: You can view, add, or remove domains via the Extension’s Options page or by right-clicking any webpage.

4. User Control

  • Manage Domains: You may add or remove domains from your list at any time via the Options interface.
  • Disable or Uninstall: You may disable or uninstall the Extension anytime through your browser’s Extensions page (chrome://extensions).

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Extension after updates constitutes acceptance of the new terms.

7. Additional Notes/Summary

This Extension was built with a strong commitment to privacy.

  • All functionality is self-contained and operates entirely on-device.
  • There is no telemetry, analytics, or usage tracking.
  • The only stored information is non-sensitive, user-controlled, and domain-specific.
